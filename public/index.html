Why Us

We are inspired by the UAE Government growth mindset approach, especially by its primary aim: enhancing citizen’s happiness through the correct implementation of technology.

Over the last 5 years, we’ve analyzed over 500 projects, assisted in and built a wide variety of solutions, from a digital bank in Germany and a pilot CBDC system for the National Bank of Ukraine to a blockchain-based voting system for a democratic political party.

 Since then we have already paved our way to create a technology that makes blockchain simple for regular people while offering new benefits for the government and enterprises (data integrity, authenticity, transparency, etc.). We aim to contribute to the implementation of the Emirates Blockchain 2021 and The Dubai Blockchain Strategies by using our expertise and best technological practices.

Our Research

At the present time, people are under threat of being controlled by major centralized digital systems. Decentralization is an alternative solver of a variety of humanity’s challenges, in particular ensuring the freedom of access to public data and the transparency of accounting processes. The study gives an insight into the capabilities of blockchain technology, principles of operation of decentralized accounting systems as well as features of reaching consensus in a decentralized environment
